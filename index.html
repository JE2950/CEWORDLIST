Cases & their uses

CaseMain uses
NominativeSubject of the verb
VocativeAddressing someone directly
AccusativeDirect object; with most prepositions; extent of time/space
GenitivePossession ("of") — domus regis = the king's house
DativeIndirect object ("to/for"); with certain verbs
AblativeInstrument/manner ("by/with"); separation ("from"); with prepositions; ablative absolute
